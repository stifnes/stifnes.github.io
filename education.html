Education

Education

Studying at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute

I started my engineering at Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology in 2015, where I was studying mechanical engineering. I completed my graduation and wanted to pursue a career in designing machines but after looking for a creative job in that industry for more than one year, I could not find what I was looking for. I started learning web developement as a side hustle and found it very interesting and innovative and decided to switch my professional career from mechanical to software engineering.

Studying at TU Dublin

After working as a front end web engineer for quite a few years in Pakistan, I was always lacking behind because of not having the core knowledge of software design and engineering, that is when I decided to pursue education in Computer Science and was lucky enough to get admission in TU Dublin, Ireland in a qualifier course which leads to masters in Computer Science.
